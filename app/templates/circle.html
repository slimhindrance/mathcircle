How to run a math circle

  1. Warm up together — a quick game (Race to 20, Target 7, Pig).
  2. One puzzle, mixed levels — older child explains; younger spots patterns.
  3. One hands-on activity — paper folding, tangrams, ten-frames.
  4. One story problem — both attempt; compare strategies.
  5. Wrap with a question — "What surprised you tonight?"

It's not a quiz. It's a conversation about ideas.
