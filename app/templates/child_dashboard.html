✨ Try the AI weekly digest?

Once a week, an AI tutor (Anthropic's Claude Haiku 4.5, running through Amazon Bedrock) can read {{ child.name }}'s practice from the past seven days and write a short note for you: what strategies they used, what they're working on, what to try next.

What gets sent? (read this before deciding)
  • Problem titles, prompts, and strands {{ child.name }} attempted in the past 7 days.
  • Whether each attempt was correct, the parent rating (easy / good struggle / too hard), time spent, and any strategy notes you typed.
  • Parent notes you wrote in the past 7 days.
  • {{ child.name }}'s name is replaced with "Child" before sending — the model never sees their real name.
  • Bedrock's contract: prompts and responses are not used to train models, do not leave the AWS region (us-east-1), and are not seen by any third party.
  • You can turn this off any time. Past digests stay; new ones won't be generated.
