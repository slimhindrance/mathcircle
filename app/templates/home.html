What a session looks like

  • ☀️ Warm-up: mental math (a few quick rounds to wake up the brain)
  • 🧩 Rich puzzle: one juicy problem worth talking through
  • 🖐️ Visual / hands-on: ten-frames, blocks, paper folding, tangrams
  • 📖 Story problem: a small situation to model and solve
  • 💬 Explain: "How do you know?" — the most important question in math
  • 🏡 Parent extension: something you can play later with toys, coins, or food
